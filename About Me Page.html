Who is Parisa Saqib?

Parisa Saqib

Settlement Assistant

Parisa Saqib is a passionate IT student at the American University of Afghanistan (AUAF). She has graduated from Zarghoona High School.
During high school, she has also taken part in many extracurricular, volunteering and academic activities. Since 2017, she has been a participant of the Young Women Leadership Conference (YWCL), Afghanistan Winter Camp (AWC) and Youth Speak Forum (YSF). She has also worked as director and cameraman of a drug video project in cooperation with Sri Lanka, as well as participated in story sharing projects with Indian schools. She enjoys reading and writing novels in her free time.
